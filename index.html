Sail the 17th-century Caribbean, commanding your crew through eight thrilling seasons of raids, duels, and adventure. Plunder treasure ships, brave deadly storms, and uncover ancient secrets to forge your legend among pirates.

Buccaneer Heroes is a fast, highly replayable print-and-play sandbox adventure with streamlined mechanics for solo and multiplayer. Pool dice to cooperate or compete as you sail the open seas on your own sheet, sharing a living world where actions and story events affect everyone.
